source file->.java=>compiler->.class
.class=>class loader->JVM
JVM=>java API(Libraries)=>bytecode verifier and JIT(Just In Time)Compiler
->binary/machine language specific to OS=>Hardware and OS


Conventions and Rules

decimal values:
	float (upto 6 precisions after the decimal)
	0.123456
	
	double (upto 12 precisions after the decimal)
	0.123456789123

Conditional Execution/Conditional Constructs

	if condition evaluates to true then it will execute one block of code 
	else it will execute the another block
